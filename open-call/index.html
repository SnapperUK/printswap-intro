Upload before 1 May

How to enter

  1. Download the Print Swap app. iOS only for now.
  2. Create your profile. Takes a couple of minutes.
  3. Upload at least one image. Choose the image you'd want a fellow photographer to live with. The app handles size, paper, and the rest.
  4. Upload before 1 May 2026. Every image uploaded before the deadline is eligible for selection.

Print specifications

There are no special requirements for this open call. Any image you upload through the Print Swap app is eligible. The app handles file format, resolution, paper, and size when you upload.

If your image is selected, The Print Space produces the exhibition print to gallery standard.

Selection

The 50 prints are selected by the Print Swap team. The question we're asking is simple: is this an image a fellow photographer would be genuinely excited to swap for one of their own? We're looking for pictures that reward being held — images with presence, intention, and a point of view. No genre requirements. Photography, illustration, and printmaking all welcome.

Selected artists are notified by 3 May 2026.

If your image isn't selected, your upload stays live on your Print Swap profile and is available for swaps from launch on 7 May.

What happens next

Most photographers don't actually want their own work on their walls. They want other people's — images by photographers whose eye they respect, pictures they'd want to look at every day.

Print Swap is built for that. Once it opens on 7 May, other photographers will browse what you've made. When one of them decides your image is worth hanging on their wall — and you feel the same about theirs — the exchange begins. Both artists offer something of their own. Both receive something they actually wanted. No buying, no selling. Just two people who looked at each other's work and said yes.

Print Swap was founded by Sam Faulkner, photographer.

Frequently asked questions

Is it really free?

Yes. Uploading and being considered for the exhibition costs nothing. If your image is picked, the exhibition print is produced and displayed at no cost to you — covered by Print Swap and The Print Space.

Do I need to print anything myself?

No. There's nothing to produce or post in. You upload your image to the Print Swap app and that's the entry.

Is Print Swap only available on iOS?

Yes, for now. Android isn't available yet.

Where is the exhibition?

The Print Space, 74 Kingsland Road, London E2 8DL. 7 May 2026. View on Google Maps.

When will I find out if I've been selected?

Selected artists are notified by 3 May 2026.

Can I enter from outside the UK?

Yes. The open call is worldwide.

What if I'm not selected?

Your upload stays live on your Print Swap profile and is available for swaps from launch on 7 May.
