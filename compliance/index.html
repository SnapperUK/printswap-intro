How Print Swap operates

Print Swap is a platform for arranging artist-to-artist print exchanges. This page explains the standards, partners, and safeguards that support the platform, including fulfilment, payments, content rules, and reporting.

Print fulfilment

Physical prints exchanged through Print Swap are produced and shipped through our nominated Print Partner.

Current Print Partner: The Print Space

The Print Partner may change from time to time. Where it does, we will update this page.

Payments

Payments made through Print Swap are processed using Stripe, a secure third-party payment processor.

Depending on the stage of the swap, the payment flow may involve:

  • pre-authorisation
  • capture on acceptance
  • fraud or risk checks
  • reversal or release of payment holds where appropriate

Print Swap does not store full card details.

Artwork rights and uploads

Artists must only upload work they have the right to use and make available through the Platform.

By uploading artwork, users confirm that:

  • they own the work or control the necessary rights
  • the upload does not infringe third-party rights
  • the work complies with our Terms and platform rules

If we become aware of an infringement claim or a breach of our content rules, we may remove the work and take further action, including account suspension.

Prohibited content

The following are not permitted on Print Swap:

  • unlawful content
  • copyright-infringing material
  • hate or extremist content
  • child sexual abuse material
  • non-consensual sexual imagery
  • obscene or unlawful violent material
  • malware or attempts to interfere with the Platform
  • other content or conduct that breaches our Terms or applicable law

We may remove content, suspend activity, or close accounts where necessary.

Moderation and enforcement

Print Swap may use a combination of:

  • automated tools (including AI-assisted content review)
  • human review
  • user reports
  • fraud and risk signals

to identify prohibited content, abuse, technical misuse, or unlawful activity.

Automated systems assist with initial screening and triage; significant decisions involve human review.

Where appropriate, we may:

  • remove content
  • pause or cancel swaps
  • freeze or close accounts
  • retain evidence
  • cooperate with payment processors, advisers, or authorities

Reporting concerns

If you want to report prohibited content, suspected infringement, abuse, fraud, or another concern, please contact hello@print-swap.com or use the in-app reporting and support tools where available.

Please include as much relevant detail as possible so we can investigate efficiently.

Delivery and print quality issues

If a print is not delivered, arrives visibly damaged, or appears to contain a significant print quality defect, contact Print Swap promptly at hello@print-swap.com.

Depending on the issue and the available evidence, Print Swap may investigate and, where appropriate, coordinate a reprint, replacement shipment, refund, credit, or another reasonable remedy.

Full details are set out in our Terms and Conditions (in the app under Settings → Legal).

Fraud, misuse, and platform protection

Print Swap may pause, reverse, refuse, or investigate activity where reasonably necessary to:

  • prevent fraud or abuse
  • correct technical or payment errors
  • protect the Platform and its users
  • comply with law or payment processor requirements

These protections are part of how we keep the platform workable and trustworthy.

Policy updates

We may update this page from time to time to reflect changes to partners, processes, or platform rules. The current Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy govern use of the Platform.
