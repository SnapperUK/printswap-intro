Most of our work now lives on screens.

Print Swap is built around putting it back on walls.

No buying or selling between artists. Two artists choose each other's work.

Founded by Sam Faulkner, photographer, and Simon Roberts, photographer — two people who wanted a direct way to exchange work without selling it and without chasing metrics.

How it works

  1. Upload your work. Photograph, print size, paper type.
  2. Browse the collection. Find work you'd clear a wall for.
  3. Propose a swap. The other artist sees your work and decides.
  4. If it's mutual, the exchange begins. Both prints are produced and dispatched.

Each artist covers the cost of their own outgoing print.
The Print Space handles production and fulfilment.
