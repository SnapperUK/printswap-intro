Most of our work now lives on screens.

Print Swap is built around putting it back on walls.

No buying or selling between artists. Two artists choose each other's work.

Print Swap started with a conversation between photographers Sam Faulkner and Simon Roberts, who wanted a way to exchange work directly without selling it and without chasing metrics.

How it works

  1. Upload your work.
  2. Browse the collection.
  3. Propose a swap.
  4. If it's mutual, the exchange begins.

Each artist covers the cost of their own outgoing print.
The Print Space handles production and fulfilment.
